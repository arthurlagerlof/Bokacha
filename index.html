About Bokachá

Bokachá started in 2019 by two students who love Turku and wanted to bring a taste of their childhood to the city.

We serve bubble tea and authentic Vietnamese coffee — simple, fun, and full of flavor. Every drink is made with care and a bit of our story behind it.
